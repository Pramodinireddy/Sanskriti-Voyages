Odisha

Odisha, located on India’s eastern coast, is a land of ancient temples, serene beaches, and tribal heritage. The majestic Konark Sun Temple and Jagannath Temple in Puri reflect its deep spiritual roots and architectural brilliance.

Beyond spirituality, Odisha offers untouched natural beauty—from the tranquil Chilika Lake with its flocks of migratory birds to the lush green forests of Simlipal. Rich in festivals, crafts, and dance forms like Odissi, Odisha is an underrated cultural gem.
