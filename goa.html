Goa

Goa is India’s beach paradise, famous for its golden sands, crystal-clear waters, and laid-back vibe. From lively beaches like Baga and Anjuna to peaceful stretches like Palolem, it’s a haven for sun-seekers and party lovers alike.

Beyond the coastline, Goa offers rich Portuguese heritage, colorful flea markets, and spice plantations. Whether you want to explore ancient forts or unwind by the sea with seafood and music, Goa is a perfect escape.
