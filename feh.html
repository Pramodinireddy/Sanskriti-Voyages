Nestled quietly on the edge of a dense forest, Forest Edge Homestay is a peaceful place to slow down and reconnect with nature. With chirping birds, fluttering butterflies, and lush surroundings, it’s a sanctuary for those seeking a digital detox.

Guests can enjoy farm-fresh meals, evening bonfires, or strolls along leafy paths. Simple yet comfortable, it’s a homestay that feels like a home—ideal for weekend getaways, writing retreats, or quiet family vacations.
